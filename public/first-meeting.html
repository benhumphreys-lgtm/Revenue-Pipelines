Definition trade-off — strict vs broader

DefinitionFilterCaptures
Strict (HubSpot native, this tracker's primary)hs_sa_first_engagement_object_type = MEETING_EVENTPure cold first meetings — no prior outreach
BroaderEarliest meeting per contact, regardless of prior emails/callsAny first meeting, even after warm-up email
Why broader is bigger: Group dinners and on-site events often have attendees who received cold-outreach emails first, so HubSpot flags their first engagement as EMAIL. Operationally they had their first meeting at the event. Exact broader count requires a custom HubSpot report (instructions in the HubSpot Build Playbook).

Operating procedure — weekly cadence

1. Monday morning: Refresh this artifact. The "Booked this week" KPI (leading indicator) and "Happening this week" KPI (lagging) recompute automatically against the new week. Compare to last week → log the delta.
2. Tuesday review: Open HubSpot saved list views. Cross-check that the source attribution matches expectations.
3. End of week: Capture the WoW snapshot if needed for the leadership update.
4. Monthly: Pull a 30-day rollup → conversion rate by source → report to leadership. Trigger reviews if any source drops >25% WoW.
