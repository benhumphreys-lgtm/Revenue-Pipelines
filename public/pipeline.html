How this is scored

Per-rep target: $1.33M ARR ($4M gap ÷ 3 active reps) by Dec 31, 2026.

Coverage ratio = sum of open deal amounts ÷ rep target. Industry standard: <4x = Red, 4x–6x = Yellow, >6x = Green.

Weighted forecast = sum of (deal amount × HubSpot deal_close_probability) ÷ rep target. Uses the rep-maintained close probability per deal, not the stage default. <100% = Red, 100%–150% = Yellow, >150% = Green.

Combined confidence: both dimensions must be Yellow or better to qualify as "On Track." A rep with high coverage but low weighted = lots of stuck or low-probability deals; a rep with low coverage but high weighted = thin pipe with no margin for slippage.

Deal count translation: Expected wins = weighted forecast ÷ $480K (Dan's $40K/mo × 12 avg deal size). Per-rep target = 3 deals, team target = 9 deals across 3 reps.

Quarterly pacing: Open deals bucketed by closedate quarter. Pre-Q2 2026 closedates are flagged as stale (shown separately) and excluded from pacing counts.
